    16 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Next week’s episode of WWE SmackDown was taped last night in preparation for the company’s travel to Australia for next weekend’s Elimination Chamber premium live event.

    Here are the spoilers for next week’s episode of WWE SmackDown (courtesy of PWInsider):

    • Bianca Belair was ringside for the match. Belair collided with Morgan, which led to Stratton winning the match with a cradle roll-up pin.
    • Breakker won a quick squash match.
    • Bate and Dunne get the win with a double powerbomb on McDonagh. Finn Balor and Damian Priest try to attack following the match, but Dunne and Bate escape.
    • Akam and Rezar win the powerbomb/neckbreaker combination. Kross attacked and injured Lashley’s arm following the match.
    • Everyone from the Men’s Elimination Chamber ends up getting involved. Randy Orton is the last man standing after hitting Drew McIntyre with an RKO.
