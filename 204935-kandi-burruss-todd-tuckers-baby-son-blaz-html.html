    Singer Kandi Burruss recently jumped on social media to show off her cute kids with her husband, Todd Tucker. The "Real Housewives of Atlanta" cast's recent post on Instagram is a photo of her husband holding his two children with her, Ace and Blaze.

    The couple's 4-month-old daughter, Blaze, looked adorable in the photo as she held a finger of her elder brother's hand. Kandi captioned the sweet Instagram photo with a comment about her husband, Todd,

    "He always gets the good pics because I'm the photographer...."

    Earlier this month, Burruss opened up about an emotional scene from the RHOA episode released on April 5, when she and her husband had an argument.

    Kandi spoke about the scene with Todd on her Youtube Channel where she goes over happenings in episodes of the Bravo reality television series.

    In the video, Tucker explained that his struggle with Kandi was that she was putting her work and career above her family in many instances. He added,

    "It's like you're gone, but when you come [home], you're not gonna be here."

    Kandi Burruss & Todd Tucker at the premiere of Apple TV+'s 'Truth Be Told' on Nov. 11, 2019 in California | Photo: Getty Images

    Tucker also reiterated the touchy scene and told his wife that she wasn't ready to have a family if she wasn't ready to prioritize him and their children. Burruss agreed that she had been exhausting herself on the job, especially during that weekend, and went on to admit her wrongdoing.

    Notwithstanding, the two lovebirds appear to be in it to win it. Most recently, Burrus and her husband, Todd, were revealed to gave agreed to attend marriage counseling for resolving their marriage hitches.

    The duo, who appeared on the April 12th episode of RHOA, sat with a marriage counselor on the show and went over Kandi's split time between her career and her family.

    She explained that Tucker handles his gifts towards his older daughter as if they were rewards for the responsibility displayed by the young lady in special conditions.

    Kandi opted for a lesser strict approach towards Todd's parenting over his daughter. She, however, added that she respects her husband's position as Kaela's birth parent and that he has the ultimate say on the matter.
