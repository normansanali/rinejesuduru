    Billie Eilish Shows off Slim Body, Glimpse of Her Hip Tattoo &amp; Navel Ring in New Clip

    Billie Eilish | Billie Eilish | Source: Getty Images | instagram.com/billieeilish

    Billie Eilish's fitness journey showed progress as she flaunted a slimmer figure in a newly shared video. The singer admitted to paying more attention to her fitness starting last year.

    On May 25, 2023, Grammy-winning songstress Billie Eilish made waves on Instagram with a brief clip revealing her newly svelte figure.

    Filmed in what seems like a dressing room, Eilish briefly bared her midriff, clad in a Renaissance art top.

    The artist's dragon tattoo subtly peeked out between her cropped top and low-rise jeans. As she struck a pose, another intriguing element of her body art — a navel ring — came into view.

    Eilish previously confessed to having three tattoos. She proudly sports an "Eilish" inscription on her sternum, proclaiming, "Yes, I love myself," when she talked about it.

    The "big boy" dragon tattoo graces her thigh, and a delicate series of fairies decorate her hand, despite initially adamant on keeping her body art hidden.

    Billie Eilish attends The 2022 Met Gala Celebrating "In America: An Anthology of Fashion" at The Metropolitan Museum of Art on May 02, 2022 in New York City | Source: Getty Images

    Eilish, a newly-minted gym enthusiast, recently spoke about her transformed approach to physical fitness. Driven by her desire to stay fit during her performances, she decided to prioritize her physical health.

    "I have been working on my bones and my body," Eilish shared in 2022. "And more seriously, for the last, like, four months, I've been completely changing the way that my life is involving fitness. Like I'm a gym rat now."

    Living almost carelessly when it came to her body, Eilish vowed to take better care of her health.

    The 21-year-old singer recently navigated a breakup with Jesse Rutherford, 31. The pair seemingly parted ways and haven't been spotted together since Coachella in April.

    Following their lowkey presence at the Indio, California music festival, the "Ocean Eyes" crooner attended the Met Gala in May without Rutherford.

    However, Eilish is poised to bounce back in her music. In late 2022, the acclaimed artist unveiled plans for new music in collaboration with her brother Finneas O'Connell, signaling a promising new album on the horizon.

    Billie Eilish and Finneas O'Connell hold the award for Best Music (Original Song) for "No Time to Die" as they attend the 2022 Vanity Fair Oscar Party following the 94th Oscars at the The Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts in Beverly Hills, California | Source: Getty Images

    Despite personal ups and downs, it's clear that Eilish is committed to her craft and her fans, marching to the beat of her own drum both in music and in life.
