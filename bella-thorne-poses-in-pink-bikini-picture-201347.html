    Pretty in pink! Bella Thorne, star of Disney Channel's Shake It Up, sunbathed in a bikini with friends in Santa Monica, Calif. on Monday, July 1.

    Wearing a bright pink string bikini, the 15-year-old looked relaxed sans makeup, keeping her red hair in a loose bun. Later, the Disney queen was seen sipping on an iced fruit smoothie drink and wearing multi-colored sunglasses while chatting with her girlfriends. She also took a dip in the pool with boyfriend, high school student Tristan Klier. 

    "Laugh at least 10 x today…it'll be a great day!" she tweeted during the day of fun in the sun.

    In a 2011 interview with Teen.com, the actress/singer shared how she stays fit and bikini-ready. "I love dancing, it's so much fun and a great way to stay healthy," she said. "Besides dance, I like to do leg lifts — I'm really strong."
