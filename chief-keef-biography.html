    Who is Chief Keef?

    Keith Farrell Cozart, commonly known as Chief Keef is an American rapper and record producer. He released his debut album ‘Finally Rich’ on December 18, 2012.

    Chief Keef’s Early Life, Childhood, Education

    Keith was born in Chicago, Illinois on August 15, 1995, to parents Alfonso Cozart and Lolita Carter.

    Throughout most of his childhood years, he lived at the Parkway Garden Homes in the Washington Park neighborhood.

    He began rapping in his childhood years. He is of American nationality. Furthermore, he belongs to African-American ethnic background.

    Talking about his education, Keith attended Dulles Elementary School and the Banner School. Later, he dropped out of Dyett High School at just age 15.

    Chief Keef’s Career, Profession

    Keith initially received local recognition with his mixtapes, ‘The Glory Road’ and ‘Bang’. After being arrested, during his house arrest, he posted several videos to his YouTube account.

    Eventually, he signed with Interscope Records. Later, he released his debut studio album ‘Finally Rich’ on December 18, 2012.

    Keith has since then released other two studio albums ‘Bang 3, Part 1’ and ‘Bang 3, Part 2’. He tweeted that he was retiring from rapping in March 2016.

    However, later in the year, he was featured in MGK’s song. Furthermore, on December 1, 2017, he released his third album, ‘Dedication’. Additionally, Keith has 2 credits for the soundtrack, 1 credit as a writer, and 1 credit as a composer.

    On Lil Uzi vert’s second studio album, Eternal Atake, in March 2020, he received his first significant production credit with the track Chrome Heart Tags.

    How much is the net worth of Chief Keef?

    Keith has not revealed his current salary. However, he has an estimated net worth of around $1 million as of 2023.

    Chief Keef’s Rumors, Controversy

    Keith has been linked with several rumors throughout his career. Over the years, he has been rumored to have been involved in relationships with Selena Gomez and Nastassia Smith.

    Furthermore, he has been involved in several controversies over the years.

    He has become part of numerous controversies due to his hip-hop feuds. Similarly, he sparked controversies after he uploaded an obscene photograph on Instagram on September 15, 2012.

    Additionally, he has also been involved in various legal issues.

    Chief Keef’s Body Measurements

    Talking about his body measurement, Keith has a height of 6 ft. Additionally, he weighs around 70 kg or 167.5 pounds.

    Furthermore, his hair color is black and his eye color is dark brown.
