    Dolph Lundgren Reveals What It Was Like Working with Amber Heard on Aquaman 2

    Dolph Lundgren is speaking out about his Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom co-star Amber Heard. In an interview with ET about the film, the 64-year-old actor opened up about working on the sequel while also filming The Expendables 4 in Europe last summer.

    29 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Dolph Lundgren is speaking out about his Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom co-star Amber Heard.

    In an interview with ET about the film, the 64-year-old actor opened up about working on the sequel while also filming The Expendables 4 in Europe last summer.

    Click inside to read more…

    “I haven’t spoken to Amber,” Dolph said. “I mean she was great during the shooting.” He added that based on what he’s heard, the film is “looking great.”

    He said of both Aquaman and The Expendables, “I was lucky to do both pictures,” he said. “It’s going to be, you know, big ass kicking.”

    Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom is set hit theaters in March 2023. There’s been some speculation about Amber‘s current involvement in the sequel in the aftermath of her six-week long defamation trial against her ex-husband Johnny Depp.

    During the trial, the 36-year-old actress admitted that she did not know if her character would make the final cut of the film. Walter Hamada, the head of the DC films segment of Warner Bros., also shared that they had considered recasting her during his testimony.
