    Harry Styles’ 21st Birthday Party: Nadine Leopold, Kendall Jenner & More

    At 21, Harry Styles is actually pretty mature. He rang in his big day with new love Nadine Leopold, 21, by his side, but he also invited ex-flings Cara Delevingne and Kendall Jenner. There didn’t seem to be an ounce of drama at the party so we guess there are no hard feelings! Perhaps that’s because Harry is totally enamored of stunning model Nadine!

    In fact, Nadine is the whole reason why Harry threw his birthday party in LA!

    “Harry has spent virtually all his free time in Los Angeles with Nadine,” a source shared with The Daily Mail. “They’re infatuated with each other and can’t stand being apart. There’s nobody else he’d rather be with on his birthday.”

    Aww so sweet! It sounds like these two are really falling for each other!

    Harry Styles Parties With Hollywood A-Listers

    Harry also celebrated with party guests Gigi Hadid, Samantha Ronson, Rashida Jones, Kelly Osbourne, David Beckham, Courteney Cox, Cindy Crawford, Adele and many, many more famous faces — including on-again couple Jennifer Lawrence and Chris Martin!

    The extravagant bash reportedly cost more than $300,000 to put on, according to the UK’s Daily Mail, and it included four Harry facecakes! We’d like a slice of one of those!

    “When he blew out his birthday candles, he FaceTimed his mom,” a source tells People magazine. “He was so sweet to all his guests.” Aww!

    Did we mention that Harry looked handsome as ever in a black blazer and a black button down shirt that showed off the ink on his chest? Happy birthday, Harry!

    So HollywoodLifers, do YOU think Harry’s party looked fun? Let us know what you think in the comments below!
