    Irina Shayk went monochrome while on mommy duty.

    Yesterday, the supermodel was photographed out and about in New York City with her ex-boyfriend, actor and director Bradley Cooper, and their three-year-old daughter, Lea De Seine Shayk Cooper. For the family outing, Shayk wore a beige leather minidress by Nanushka paired with matching calf-high chunky platform boots from her Tamara Mellon collaboration. The model accessorized her look with a coordinating Burberry leather shoulder bag and matching oversized sunglasses. Cooper was dressed casual for the occasion, sporting a black graphic Willie Nelson T-shirt, navy trousers, sneakers, and sunglasses.

    In March, Shayk opened up to ELLE about how she manages to raise her daughter with her ex and why she detests the term co-parenting.

    "I never understood the term co-parenting. When I'm with my daughter, I'm 100 percent a mother, and when she's with her dad, he's 100 percent her dad," Shayk told the magazine. "Co-parenting is parenting."

    She also expressed why she rarely addresses her personal life in interviews and how she works to avoid tabloid coverage of herself and her family.

    "My past relationship, it's something that belongs to me, and it's private," Shayk explained. "It's just a piece of my inner self that I don't want to give away."

    She added, "I don't read what is out there. Honestly, I'm too busy raising a child. If they want to write articles [about me], they're doing their job. I'm concentrating on my life and my friends. The rest is just noise."
