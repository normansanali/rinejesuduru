    Know More About Jessica Lucas

    Jessica Lucas began performing at the age of seven and studied at Toronto’s Children’s Arts Theatre School.

    She began her career by performing in local productions of Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs, Grease, Cinderella, Mousetrap, and Music Medley.

    Jessica first appeared in the Canadian adolescent drama television series Edgemont in 2001 as a recurring character, Bekka Lawrence. In 2004, Jessica appeared as Roxanne in an episode of The L Word.

    She joined the cast of CSI: Crime Scene Investigation in 2007 as Ronnie Lake, a recurring character. Jessica appeared as Kimberly MacIntyre in four episodes of The CW’s 90210 in 2008.
