    Jack Donnelly (born October 28, 1985) is a British Actor, Professional Model, Theatre Artist and Businessman from Bournemouth, England. He is the founder of “Chuckle Duster“, a London based Comedy group. He is famously known as “Malin Åkerman’s husband“, Canadian-Swedish actress and model.

    Movies, Career & Filmography

    The 33-year-old Actor has made his acting debut from Nickelodeon’s series named “House of Anubis” in 2011. In that TV show, he has played the role of “Jason Winkler” and appeared in 31 episodes of it.

    Furthermore, Donnelly has also seen in the guest role of TV shows like “Threesome”, “Misfits”, “Dancing on the Edge” and “Doctors”.

    In 2013, Jack got his lucky break to play the lead role of “Jason” in “Atlantis” British fantasy TV series. He is also appreciated for his work in this show.

    After this, he has appeared in his first movie “United We Fall” as “Olly Hunter” in 2014. However, this film does not do well in the box office. In 2017, his film “A Royal Winter” was released worldwide, where his fans loved his role as “Prince Adrian”.

    Currently, he is busy in the shooting of his upcoming comedy movie named “Friendsgiving“. This film is scheduled to release in 2019. He will appear as “Jeff” and his wife Malin Åkerman in the lead role of “Molly” in the Friendsgiving film.
