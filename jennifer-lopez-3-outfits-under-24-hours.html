    Jennifer Lopez Embarks on a Fashion Marathon with 3 Bold Looks

    01 Oct 2024, 00:00

    If anyone can pull off three showstopping looks in 24 hours, it's Jennifer Lopez.

    The multi-hyphenate is on a whirlwind promo tour for her new cocktail line, Delola. While jetting across the country in an Instagram series dubbed #DelolaDiaries, Lopez has put her bold superstar style front and center.

    In her first look, Lopez shimmered in an ensemble pulled from Valentino's pre-fall 2023 collection, including a flashy cerulean midi skirt covered in circular reflective sequins and a light gray jersey sweater embellished all over with matching feather trim. She upped the ante with her signature sky-high platform heels, this time a metallic iteration by Andrea Wazen, and a bejeweled silver version of Valentino's Garavani Loco handbag.

    Her day 2 look continued to favor a vibrant color palette. She opted for a furry lilac sweater, which she tucked into a high-waist gray skirt cinched at the waist by a thick black belt with a sparkling buckle. She capped off the ensemble with a violet trench coat and slouchy black leather heeled boots. Accessories included purple-tinted butterfly-shaped sunglasses and a puffy black Max Mara leather clutch.

    For her final outfit, Lopez put forth her own take on the preppy aesthetic. This included plenty of tweed in the form of a belted jacket and a flowing midi skirt in a matching gray oatmeal hue. She added a speckled black newsboy cap and dark brown knee-high leather boots. For a modern finish, she carried Bottega Veneta's black pouch bag with a large gold chain handle.
