    The Kitty Cut Hair Trend Is Set To Be All The Rage In 2024

    26 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Are you on the lookout for a chic hair transformation that’s also low maintenance? Look no further than the ‘kitty cut.’ As shorter hair trends continue to gain momentum, especially with the resurgence of the bob last year, the kitty cut strikes the perfect balance, providing more length and versatility.

    Evolving from popular styles like the wolf cut and butterfly cut, the kitty cut offers a polished and sophisticated look that blends seamlessly with the ongoing shift towards shorter, more textured hairstyles.

    What Is The Kitty Cut?

    The kitty cut is a sleek and refined take on layered hairstyles, falling between the shoulders and collarbone. Unlike its shaggier predecessors, such as the wolf cut, the kitty cut maintains subtlety while infusing sophistication through delicately cascading layers. Its charm lies in the balance between a polished appearance and a hint of feline allure, creating a trendy yet timeless haircut. It features face-framing, rounded layers and a collarbone-skimming length, making it the sleeker and more refined sister of the wolf or shag haircut.

    Styling The Kitty Cut

    This versatile cut caters to various hair textures, including straight, wavy and curly locks. To achieve the perfect kitty cut, longer layers are key, with the longest layer only a centimetre or so shorter than the base length. Ask your hairstylist to incorporate softly ascending layers in the back and around the face while avoiding overly short and choppy cuts that venture into shag territory. Optional swooping layers around the face, such as cheekbone-length curtain bangs and jaw-length pieces, add a framing effect.

    Texture-wise, the kitty cut can be worn in different styles, from air-dried to blow-dried, voluminous to half-waved, and even slightly messed up and side-parted for an added flair. The cut’s adaptability makes it a canvas for self-expression, allowing individuals to personalise their look based on their preferences and daily styling routine.

    Getting The Kitty Cut

    Achieving the perfect kitty cut involves clear communication with your hairstylist. It’s advisable to bring picture references to ensure you and your stylist are on the same page. The cut’s adaptability allows it to suit various face shapes and hair textures, making it a go-to choice for those looking for a visible change without a drastic transformation.

    Styling Tips

    Experts recommend an undone and easy styling approach for the kitty cut. Using suitable products like mousses, finishing creams and texture sprays enhances the natural texture and movement of the cut. The kitty cut is versatile across different hair textures, suitable for straight, wavy and curly hair. For curly hair, a curl-by-curl cut ensures a customised fit.

    With its polished yet playful aesthetic, this trend is set to dominate 2024, offering a fresh take on layered hairstyles that complements the ongoing shift towards shorter, textured looks. So, whether you’re growing out a bob or simply seeking a stylish change, unleash your inner feline with the ‘purr’fect kitty cut.
