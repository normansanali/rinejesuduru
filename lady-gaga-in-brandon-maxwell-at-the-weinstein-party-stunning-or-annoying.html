    12 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Here are some photos from the Weinstein Company’s Pre-Oscar Dinner 2016 at Montage Beverly Hills. Shockingly, there was a distinct lack of Marchesa on many of the big-name celebrities in attendance. Let’s start with Lady Gaga. You know what I miss? I miss when Gaga stopped being a thing. But her “comeback” is in full swing and if you couldn’t tell by her face, she’s loving every minute of it. She wore this deconstructed tuxedo dress by Brandon Maxwell. It seems like this should have been obscene, but it wasn’t. Also: Gaga’s Little Monsters have been threatening us (me) because I’m not a believer. Yeah… I will continue to not be a believer.

    John Legend and Chrissy Teigen… the red is very pretty on her, but I’m not loving the hair. While you know my feelings on bangs, I think this is one situation where a woman needs something to frame her face, like some shaggy fringe or something.

    Kerry Washington in a very simple LBD. She’s just out and about this weekend to have fun and network – she’ll be presenting at the Oscars too.

    Colin Firth and his wife Livia Giuggioli. I don’t think there’s an actor more closely associated with Harvey Weinstein, and for all of Harvey’s faults and sketchiness, I am grateful for Weinstein’s close association with Firth. I guess Colin and Livia just came out to have fun too.

    Jaime King in Cushnie et Ochs. The dress is meh-to-fine, especially for a pre-Oscar party. I think I’ve figured out what bugs me about her look though: she doesn’t know what to do with her hair. Right now, it’s too dark and the center part is unflattering.

    The Marchesa-wearing was left to Zendaya, who actually wore two Marchesa looks to the party. Neither of which was very good, if I’m being honest. But she’s young and beautiful, so she comes close to pulling off these terribly budget designs.
