    Lauren Harpe On Survivor 44

    Lauren Harpe shouldn’t be mistaken as a feeble competitor just because she is a cute face, a sweet demeanor, and a modest stature. She signed up on Survivor to have a challenge for herself. As a single parent, she is doing it for her boys.

    She told Parade.com, “I’m a single parent, and so my day-to-day life is just going to work and taking care of my boys and running back and forth to soccer practice and different things like that. I recently got a divorce, so I’ve been on a self-growth journey just to see what I’m capable of. So this is really for me and to see all that I can do.”

    Lauren started watching the show with her father when she was very young. However, life happened so did break from watching the show. Now she calls herself the new era superfan. She added, “I’ve been watching these most recent seasons and binge-watching previous seasons as well. But I’m more of a new era superfan which I think is going to be really an asset for me in this game.”

    For her, the winner and non-winner, Lauren picked Parvati and Shan in that respect. So what prepared her for the show? The answer was her divorce and becoming a single mother. the process taught her how to take care of herself and her boys. She added, “It was physically, mentally, emotionally, and financially taxing on my body. I’ve been through a lot mentally, and that’s going to prepare me for all of the challenges that are going to come my way in this game.”

    Is Lauren Harpe Dating Anyone?

    Per her social media, Lauren Harpe is currently single. She is still a single mother to two boys called CJ and Braxton and not romantically involved with anyone as of this article.

    In an Instagram post on her life, Lauren discussed how she first got pregnant at age 21 along with her partner, who she had been dating for six months at the time. The young mother had to leave her final year of nursing school, but the two eventually got married. Lauren did her best to make the best of the situation, despite the unfavorable aspects of her pregnancy and schooling.

    “My husband and I jumped from apartment to apartment, and most of the time didn’t have the funds to pay for our own rent or even buy groceries. We were irresponsible, living a facade, and broke. To make a long story short, my marriage failed after about 7 years. We divorced, and I became a single mother,” Lauren shared about her marriage.

    How Old Is Lauren Harpe?

    At the time of the filming of the show, Lauren Harpe was 31 years old. She celebrates her birthday on 30 July.

    Who Are Lauren Harpe Parents?

    Lauren Harpe is the daughter of Darrell and Cheryl Siemens. According to Cheryl’s Facebook, she and her husband have been married since 1985. Darrell attended Lincoln High School in Port Arthur, Texas. Per the LinkedIn that likely belongs to him, he works at Chevron Phillips Chemical Company as an Operating Assistant, Logistics Coordinator – Aromatics and Boiler Area, and Marine Traffic Movements.

    Cheryl has stated on her FB bio that she is a biology teacher.

    In 2023, Darrell is 62 years old whereas Cheryl turned 63 in November 2022. They are both currently based in Port Arthur, Texas.

    Lauren Harpe Job

    Lauren Harpe is currently working as a fourth-grade teacher at Barbers Hill. Per her LinkedIn, she was an English teacher at Beaumont Independent School District.

    Lauren completed her college education at Lamar University in 2014. She enrolled in the Humanities program and gained university credits as a high school junior.

    In 2012, she went back to school and took part in a number of extracurricular activities. She was named the 2014 Honoree for Most Promising Health Professional-College of Education and Human Development in addition to being a member of Eta Sigma Gamma. Lauren earned a Bachelor of Science in Health in 2014 and was named to the President’s List during graduation.

    Lauren began working at ET’s Childcare in May 2013 as a Youth Camp Administrator, where she remained until August 2013. From January to May 2014, she also served as an intern with Beaumont Health Department’s Health Promotion Division. The reality TV personality began working as a substitute teacher for Port Arthur Independent School District (PAISD) in August 2014, shortly after graduating. She received a promotion to Academic Tutor in November of the same year.

    Lauren left PAISD in April 2015 and joined Beaumont Independent School District as a Teacher in May 2015. The reality TV star remained affiliated with the institute until November 2018, when she switched over to Lewisville ISD. In July 2019, the educator also became a part of VIPKid to teach through the internet. Ultimately, Lauren left Lewisville ISD in July 2020 and became a part of Barbers Hill Independent School District as a Teacher.
