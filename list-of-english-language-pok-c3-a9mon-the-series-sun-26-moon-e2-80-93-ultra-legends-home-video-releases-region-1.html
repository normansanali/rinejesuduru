    Three Pokémon the Series: Sun & Moon – Ultra Legends Collections released by VIZ Media and distributed through Warner Home Video in 2020 and 2021, containing the whole season between them.

    Pokémon the Series: Sun & Moon – Ultra Legends - The Last Grand Trial

    Pokémon the Series: Sun & Moon – Ultra Legends - The Last Grand Trial was released on September 8, 2020 by VIZ Media and distributed through Warner Home Video. It contains two discs, with 18 episodes across them combined. Optional English closed captioning is included.

    Clean versions of the opening and ending themes are included as a special feature.

    Pokémon the Series: Sun & Moon – Ultra Legends - The Alola League Begins!

    Pokémon the Series: Sun & Moon – Ultra Legends - The Alola League Begins was released on January 12, 2021 by VIZ Media and distributed through Warner Home Video. It contains two discs, with 18 episodes across them combined. Optional English closed captioning is included.

    Clean versions of the opening and ending themes are included as a special feature.

    Contents

    Pokémon the Series: Sun & Moon – Ultra Legends - The First Alola League Champion

    Pokémon the Series: Sun & Moon – Ultra Legends - The First Alola League Champion was released on May 4, 2021 by VIZ Media and distributed through Warner Home Video. It contains two discs, with 18 episodes across them combined. Optional English closed captioning is included.

    Clean versions of the opening and ending themes are included as a special feature.
