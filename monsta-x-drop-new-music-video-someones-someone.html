    Monbebe, guess what? Monsta X is back with another impressive music video!

    After releasing their catchy, mid-tempo track “Someone’s Someone” on Oct. 4, the septet has released the music video for their new single and it will make all their fans swoon. The video follows the group’s seven members – Shownu, Wonho, Minhyuk, Kihyun, Hyungwon, Joohoney, and I.M – as they wander around a dock and neon-lit bar wishing they had “someone” by their side. The guys sing, “We all wanna be someone’s/Wanna be someone’s, someone/Someone we can’t live without.”

    You can watch the music video for “Someone’s Someone” below.

    The chorus is undeniably catchy, and fans would instantly hear that the lyrics are entirely in English. “Someone’s Someone” follows Monsta X’s previous English-language singles, “Who Do You Love?” featuring French Montana, and “Love U.” The former is the group’s first top 40 radio hit, as “Who Do You Love?” currently sits in the top 25. They also performed the hit on The Ellen Degeneres Show on Sept. 25.

    Monsta X is certainly taking over the world and this is just the beginning for them. “Someone’s Someone” comes ahead of the guys’ next comeback, which will take place on Oct. 28. That day, Monsta X will release their new mini-album FOLLOW, as well as their new music video for “Find You.” Judging from the teasers, fans can expect Monsta X’s next music video to be really intense since it seems it will have a narrative with the guys getting into an accident.
