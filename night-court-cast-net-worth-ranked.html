    Night Court was a very popular sitcom in the 1980s. The Show first aired on NBC in 1984 and ran until 1992. Viewers loved the hysterical take on the legal system, and the actors and actresses that brought the story to life. Now, over three decades later, NBC has created a sequel series of the beloved show. This follows the trend in television of rebooting or recreating older television shows.

    The original cast included Harry Anderson, John Larroquette, and more. Sadly, most of the original cast members have since passed away, so there is not much of a chance of cameos aside from Larroquette’s role in the new show. The reboot of Night Shift follows the life of Anderson’s character’s daughter Abby Stone, played by Melissa Rauch, and her job as the new night shift judge. Here is the original and new cast of Night Court ranked by net worth.

    8 How Much Was Charlie Robinson Worth?

    Charlie Robinson was among the original cast of Night Court in the 1980s. He played Macintosh “Mac” Robinson on the NBC sitcom. He amassed a net worth of somewhere between $1 and $2 million from his work on the show and afterwards.

    After Night Court ended, Robinson received many roles. He had guest appearances in numerous television shows, included but not limited to How I Met Your Mother, Grey’s Anatomy, Hart of Dixie, and NCIS. Sadly, Robinson passed away on July 11th, 2021.

    7 How Much Is Lacretta Worth?

    Lacretta is one of the newest cast members of the Night Court reboot. Though a familiar face to some, Lacretta only has eight other acting credits to her name. She had guest appearances in Law & Order: Special Victims Unit and the CW’s Gotham. This is the actress’s first role that lasts more than one episode of a television show.

    Lacretty plays Donna “Gurgs” Gurganous, the baliff for Abby’s court, in the Night Court reboot. Some report her net worth to be as low as $500,000, but there are many reports of the actress having a net worth of $5 million.

    6 How Much Is India de Beaufort Worth?

    India de Beaufort plays the Assistant District Attorney in the Night Court sequel series. Her character is named Olivia, and Beaufort has a net worth of roughly $5 million from her work in the entertainment industry.

    Beaufort has been in essentially every comedic show in recent years. She had guest roles in How I Met Your Mother and Zoey’s Extraordinary Playlist, as well as a six-episode run on the hit HBO show Veep. She recently played the role of Charlie in Netflix’s original show Firefly Lane.

    5 How Much Was Harry Anderson Worth?

    Harry Anderson was the lead of the original NBC Night Shift. He played Judge Harry T. Stone and led the sitcom for its entirety. Anderson is best known for his work on Night Shift, but he also had a role in Cheers and was the lead in the television show Dave’s World.

    Sadly, Anderson passed away in 2018. He died in his sleep of a stroke caused by influenza and heart disease in his home in North Carolina. At the time of his death, Anderson had a net worth of $5 million. His character in Night Shift lives on through Melissa Rauch, who plays his daughter Abby Stone in the reboot.

    4 How Much Is Kapil Talwalker Worth?

    Kapil Talwalker has ranging reports of his net worth. Some report it much lower than his fellow Night Court actors, while others claim he has a much higher net worth. He likely falls within the range of $4 to $6 million in regards to his financial worth.

    In the reboot of Night Court, Talwalker plays Neil, Abby Stone’s clerk. Aside from his work in the new show, he is most recognizable for his role in Zoey’s Extraordinary Playlist. Night Court is not his first time working on a reboot. Talwalker also had a role in the CW’s Charmed reboot.

    3 How Much Was Markie Post Worth?

    Markie Post was another actress from the original cast of the highly successful Night Court from the 1980s. She played Christine Sullivan in the former show. She is actually most recognizable from her role as Mary’s mother in the film There’s Something About Mary.

    Post amassed her $12 million net worth from the previously mentioned roles as well as numerous other acting jobs. She had an 18-episode arc on the popular Chicago P.D. and appeared as a guest role in Netflix’s Santa Clarita Diet. Unfortunately, Post passed away on August 7th, 2021.

    2 How Much Is Melissa Rauch Worth?

    Melissa Rauch is the star of the reboot of Night Court. She plays Abby Stone, daughter of the original show’s main character Harry T. Stone, who was played by the deceased Harry Anderson. Abby Stone has taken her father’s position as a judge.

    Before playing the lead in Night Court, Rauch was best known for playing Bernadette Rostenkowski-Wolowitz in the sitcom The Big Bang Theory. Rauch had mixed feelings about how Jim Parsons ended the television show. Rauch is married to Winston Beigel, has two children, and a net worth of $20 million.

    1 How Much Is John Larroquette Worth?

    John Larroquette has the highest net worth of his Night Court co-stars, both original and reboot. He was a member of the original cast in the 1980s, and he has come back to reprise his role as Dan Fielding in the sequel series.

    Larroquette is the only member of the original cast to be a part of the reboot. This is due to how many of the original cast members that have sadly passed away over the past 30 years. Larroquette has a reported net worth of $28 million.
