    Yadira Calito, the nanny of famed American actress and singer Katherine McPhee, was killed in a horrific vehicle accident last month on August 9.

    Calito, originally from El Salvador, was 55 years old when she passed away. She moved to the United States in 1989 and resided in Los Angeles, where she served as a nanny for several families.

    Moreover, she was recognized for her compassion, kindness, and devotion to the children she cared for.

    Calito began working for Katherine in February 2021, shortly after the birth of her son, Rennie. She soon earned their family’s confidence and affection. The actress’ nanny frequently traveled worldwide, assisting her with her hectic schedule.

    RIP Yadira Calito Accident: Cause Of Death

    Yadira Calito accident that took her life happened around 9:10 a.m. on August 9 at Hamer Toyota in the Los Angeles area. She was killed by an 84-year-old lady driving a Toyota RAV4.

    According to authorities, the elderly woman accelerated the SUV for unclear reasons. It eventually crashed, killing numerous people inside the dealership.

    Moreover, the mishap injured two customers and one staff member. Even though paramedics arrived immediately, Yadira died. She was declared deceased at the hospital.

    TMZ reported the accident when the older woman accidentally hit the throttle pedal instead of the brake while taking her car in for repair.

    Surprisingly, the Toyota RAV4 driver was not injured in the collision. She was recognized as a Mission Hills resident.

    Yadhira Calito Obituary

    Yadhira was more than a nanny; she was a member of the McPhee family, having cared for Katherine McPhee’s kid for many years. Her life was distinguished by her strength, love for family, and persistent commitment to helping others.

    Calito spent almost two decades serving countless families as a committed and caring nanny. She is survived by her fiancé, Jaime, and her children, Diana, Luis, Juan, and Pamela.

    Likewise, she has eight grandchildren, with three more on the way. She left behind a family who adored her and will remember the memories they had forever.

    According to obituaries.forestlawn.com, her funeral services were held on September 1, 2023, in Forest Lawn, Holywood Hills, Church of the Hills.

    The incident that resulted in Calito’s premature death is a sad reminder of life’s unpredictability. The news of her death elicited an outpouring of grief and condolences from the entertainment world and the general population.
