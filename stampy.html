    Who Is Stampy? Age And Biography

    Stampy is 32 years old and was born on December 13, 1990. He was born in Portsmouth, Hampshire, England, and is a Sagittarius. Stampy studied video production at university as part of his educational journey. He had planned to become a games journalist at first.

    Stampy’s interests included reading, Magic, and Pokémon. While in high school, he discovered his passion for making videos. Stampy’s early videos mainly consisted of him and his friends filming each other doing ridiculous stunts. He eventually started making videos of short fictional stories on his own. He mostly made these videos with claymation, and the name of his channel is a reference to a character who appeared in these shorts.

    How Did Stampy Pursue His Career? Professional Life

    Stampy works as a YouTuber, actor, voice actor, and author. He rose to prominence on YouTube as stampylonghead or stampylongnose. As his internet alias “Stampy Cat,” he is also a popular online gamer known for posting videos about the sandbox video game Minecraft.

    Stampy’s YouTube channel was one of the top ten most-watched in the world in 2014. Similarly, he has appeared on several CBBC and Disney television programs. Stampy and Maker Studios announced a collaboration to launch an educational app the same year.

    When the videos became popular, the YouTube star quit his job as a bartender to focus solely on producing the content. His avatar was the Stampy Cat character. It’s a cat that’s orange and white. A commercially available Minecraft skin based on the surface Fidget from the video game Dust: An Elysian Tail depicts it.

    On April 25, 2015, the YouTube star collaborated with Maker Studios to launch a spin-off educational YouTube channel.
    Wonder Quest is a scripted, professionally produced, and designed series. It can also be used in the classroom. The YouTube sensation has also been cast in a Minecraft film.

    Stampy has also agreed to a two-book deal with Egmont Publishing. Stampy’s Lovely Book was released on October 22, 2015. This book is aimed at children aged 4 to 10 and includes games, activities, jokes, and facts about Stampy Cat and his Vlogger friends. Another book, Stampy Cat: Stick with Stampy! was released on July 28, 2016. It’s a 32-page paperback with sticker-based activities, games, jokes, and posters.
