    Timothy O’Connor Obituary – In a somber moment for the Bethel Park community, the Police Department is mourning the passing of its retired chief, Timothy C. O’Connor. According to the Allegheny County Medical Examiner’s Office, Chief O’Connor breathed his last just after 11:30 p.m. on Tuesday, leaving behind a legacy of leadership and service that will be remembered for years to come.

    Chief Timothy C. O’Connor served the Bethel Park Police Department with distinction, and his untimely death has cast a pall over the close-knit law enforcement community. The department, still grappling with the news, extends its heartfelt condolences to Chief O’Connor’s family and friends, urging the public to keep them in their thoughts and prayers during this challenging period.

    O’Connor, a dedicated law enforcement professional, led the Bethel Park Police Department for nearly a decade before retiring just a month ago. His tenure was marked by a commitment to community safety, excellence in policing, and a steadfast dedication to upholding the values of justice and integrity. Under Chief O’Connor’s leadership, the department forged strong ties with the community it served.

    The suddenness of Chief O’Connor’s passing has left the community in shock, with colleagues and residents alike reflecting on the impact of his service. A man of honor and integrity, Chief O’Connor’s influence extended far beyond the confines of the police department. His legacy is one of leadership, mentorship, and a tireless commitment to the well-being of Bethel Park.
