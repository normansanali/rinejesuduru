    07 Sep 2024, 00:00

    HGTV’s new show Unfinished Business premiered on January 15, 2022, and it saw Tom Reber taking on home renovation jobs that were abandoned. But Tom isn’t just a contractor, he’s a father, an author, ex-marine, and a clothing store owner.

    This Tom Reber Bio explores everything you need to know about this amazing personality. 

    Meet Tom Reber, Unfinished Business Host

    We’ve seen quite a few homeowners trying to tackle renovations themselves until they are forced to give up their DIY dreams. So, this was where Unfinished Business stepped in.

    As its host, Tom Reber offered support, guidance, and tough love to owners needed to regain control of runaway projects.

    Tom Reber Net Worth

    Tom Reber flaunted a net worth of under $1.25 million in 2022.

    This home renovation coach worked his magic through his company The Contractor Fight then. Reportedly, he founded the renovation firm back in November 2012 and has since helped over 1000s of home improvement contractors earn “the respect and dignity equal to the value that they bring to their clients.”

    One of their clients, Cobus Breytenbach, said, “I was 30k in debt when I joined the program. Charged the fee to a credit card. Best decision I ever made!”

    Aside from managing his renovation company, Tom was also involved in the clothing business at the time. He founded Live Unafraid Apparel in Colorado Springs in the Summer of 2020, after wanting to build a clothing brand that “spoke to the fire inside people tired of hiding their light.”

    But initially, Tom started his career as a marine. He served the US Marine Corps for 4 years until he was honorably discharged in 1992.

    Penning his years of experience, Tom published a book called “Winning the Contractor Fight” in September 2021. It sold for $14.99 on Amazon.

    Also, he spread his will to “fight” over an apple podcast titled “The Contractor Fight with Tom Reber”.

    As for his education, Tom graduated from Wheaton North High School in 1987 and joined StrongFirst.

    Tom Reber Age

    Tom Reber was 52 years of when he first appeared on Unfinished Business in 2022.

    Is Tom Reber Married?

    No, Tom Reber wasn’t married as of 2022. However, the star was about to, and his fiancee’s name is Lee Unruh. The two were together at least since June 2021 and often featured each other over their IG.

    “All the people in my life know the beautiful love story I have with this man. If you don’t know ask me,” Lee shared. Here’s her IG @darkhorse2u, if you ever intend to.

    As for Tom, he shared that the “woman who stole his heart” was his “queen” and he loves her more than she will ever know. No wonder, he proposed to her in November 2021, all prepared to take her as his wife.

    Talking a little more about his fiancee, Lee (Donaldson) Unruh is a professional hunter, jumper, and equitation trainer who owned Unruh Enterprises, Inc. Reportedly, she opened the company back in August 2000 in Castle Rock, CO.

    Also, Unruh has two daughters from her past relationship named Maddie and Noel @_strange.stuff_. Her eldest daughter, Maddie @overcastkid08 turned 18, on August 23, 2021

    Likewise, this wouldn’t be Tom’s first relationship either. He has a son named Dakota Reber (who turned 21 on April 22, 2021) from his past marriage. His son, Dakota followed in his dad’s footsteps and was serving the US Marine Corps.

    Tom Reber Parents And Siblings

    Tom Reber grew up in a contractor family. His dad was a well-known contractor in Colorado, while not much was known about his mother — except she was “the most influential woman” in Tom’s life.

    Unfortunately, Tom’s mother Debbie passed away on February 6, 2021.

    It seems that Debbie and Tom’s fiance Lee were also close to each other. After her death, Lee took it to her IG to write, “Said goodbye to my “mother in love” today. e I love you so much and will miss our talks and text messages.”

    As for Tom’s siblings, he has a sister named Beth Verdico and a brother named Joe. Here’s a little info on them:

    Joe: He was happily married to his wife Tiffany, and the duo welcome their first kid Carter in May 2021.

    Beth: She was single and living in Chicago, Illinois. For living, she sold “makeup that doesn’t come off and has skincare stuff in it.”

    Tom Reber Height

    Tom Reber stands tall at a height of 5 feet 10 inches (178 cm). But he feels 6’6” when he’s with his finance. So he says.
