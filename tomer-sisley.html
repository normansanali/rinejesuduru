    Career

    Sisley did stand-up comedy for six years. In 2003, he became the first French stand-up comedian to win the world’s largest comedy festival, Just for Laughs, in Canada. Bedwin Hacker (2003), the comedy drama Virgil (2005), the French romantic comedy-drama Toi et moi (2006), the French criminal film Truands (2007), and the French action thriller Largo Winch were among his first features (2008). Sisley was named Most Promising Newcomer at the Étoiles d’or French honors in 2009 for his performance in Largo Winch.
