    THE Armani White trend went viral in 2022 following the release of his song titled Billie Eilish.

    Shortly after the track dropped, it turned into a popular TikTok trend and now users want to know how to participate.

    What is the Armani White 'Billie Eilish' TikTok trend?

    Since its release in March 2022, Armani White's song has been used on TikTok in over 618,000 videos.

    The line of the song in particular that went viral is: "I'm stylish, Glock tucked, big T-shirt, Billie Eilish."

    When the beat drops, TikTokers will suddenly reveal a new look.

    In the videos, people are seen taking off a baggy hoodie or T-shirt and transitioning into sexier outfits once the beat drops.

    Read More TikTok Trends

    Billie is known for wearing baggy outfits.

    She has said that her choice of clothes means she can't be body-shamed.

    Who is Armani White?

    White is an aspiring rapper from West Philadelphia who has released several songs over the years.

    His biggest hit so far is arguably Billie Eilish, which has since been streamed over 52 million times.

    In August 2022, he also dropped the song, Diamond Dallas.

    When he is not in the studio, he can often be found posting about his life and upcoming tunes on Instagram to over 120,000 followers.

    What has Armani White said about the trend?

    Shortly after his song went viral, White sat down with the New York Post to discuss the trend and the success he's had since its release.

    White said: “I mean, honestly, I think Billie Eilish’s style is what the song’s about, but it’s also the character that Billie Eilish’s music portrays.

    “I feel like it’s kind of identical to what I like to portray in my own music.”

    The seven-time Grammy winner is arguably best known for her unique music style and baggy clothes.

    As a result of his viral fame, White has been able to secure a record deal with Def Jam Records, a popular label behind several stars, including LL Cool J, Jay-Z, and Kanye West.

    White added: “It’s a blessing to deal with people who just believe in me and people who understand the culture.
