    R&B icon Lauryn Hill was one of the biggest stars of the 90s with huge hits like Doo Wop (That Thing), and Ex-Factor.

    But who are her children? Here’s all you need to know.

    Who are Lauryn Hill’s children?

    Lauryn Noelle Hill was born on May 26, 1975.

    She grew up in New Jersey before finding success as an actress and a musician at a young age.

    She had a recurring role on American soap opera As The World Turns before co-starring with Whoopi Goldberg in Sister Act 2 (1993).

    The 47-year-old had her first taste of chart success as part hip-hop trio Fugees, with fellow members Wyclef Jean and Pras Michael.

    She then went solo at just 22, and released an album still regarded as one of the best albums in pop history.

    The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill, released in 1998, bagged five Grammy Awards, and broke the record for first-week sales by a solo female artist in the United States.

    But after becoming an international superstar, she withdrew from the public eye.

    In 2013, she was sentenced to three months in jail for tax evasion.

    The R&B superstar has six children.

    Zion David Marley

    Lauryn Hill has six children: five with Rohan Marley, and another son, Mica, in 2011.

    Zion is Lauryn’s eldest son with Rohan.

    His name features on his mum’s debut album, in the track “To Zion”.

    In 2017, Billboard reported that Zion had become a first-time father, to son, Zephaniah.

    Selah Louise Marley

    Selah Louise is a 23-year-old singer and fashion model.

    She’s modelled in campaigns for a number of high-end fashion brands including Urban Outfitters and Beyoncé’s Ivy Park label.

    She released her first official release, an EP, Star Power, in August 2021.

    Joshua Omaru Marley.

    Joshua Marley is musician like his mum and has posted tracks on SoundCloud.

    He also has over 46k followers on Instagram, with one of his posts featuring a picture of him with his mum, and Kanye West.

    John Nesta Marley

    John is Lauryn and Rohan’s youngest son.

    Has has over 10k followers on his Instagram.

    Sarah Marley

    Little is known about Lauryn and Rohan’s youngest daughter Sarah.

    Micah

    Not much is known about Lauryn’s son Micah who was born in 2011.

    In July 25, 2011, Rohan Tweeted a message congratulating her on the new arrival.

    He wrote: “I’m forwarding all well wishes to Ms. Hill on the birth of her new son. I’m sure she’ll appreciate the love being sent to her RASTAFARI R.M.”

    It is not known who the child’s father is.

    Who is Rohan Marley?

    Lauryn began dating Rohan Marley in 1996.

    Rohan is an entrepreneur and the son of reggae icon Bob Marley.

    The 50-year-old was an American football player, and co-founded an organic farming business called Marley Coffee in 2009.

    He’s got quite a sizeable social media following, with over 487k followers on Instagram.

    Who is Lauryn Hill dating now?

    Lauryn and Rohan reportedly split in 2011.

    She is not known to be currently dating anyone.
