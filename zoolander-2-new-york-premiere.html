    For a movie as highly-anticipated (and outrageous) as Zoolander 2, a traditional premiere would not fit the bill. The cast—including Ben Stiller, Owen Wilson, Penelope Cruz and even the creative directors of Valentino—took to New York City on a purple red carpet, accompanied by a surprise fashion show. And of course, big-name models like Gigi Hadid, Stella Maxwell and Irina Shayk walked the pseudo runway in a mixture of Marc Jacobs, Alexander Wang, Valentino and Tommy Hilfiger.
